Sprinklers — Landscaping

Welcome to Green Acres Sprinklers and Landscaping, Inc., one of Utah’s most innovative Landscape Design and Sprinkler Installation companies offering services in Salt Lake County, Utah County, Wasatch County and Summit County. We have completed projects in Park City, Midway, Heber, Alpine, Highland, American Fork, Lehi, Draper, Sandy, South Jordan, Riverton, Holladay, Herriman and more. Our signature Trenchless Sprinkler Installation and Personalized Landscape Designs are distinctive, functional, and simply magnificent. We specialize in all aspects of exterior design and installation with an emphasis on quality workmanship, professionalism and meticulous attention to detail.
